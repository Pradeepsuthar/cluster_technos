KPO Services

(KNOWLEDGE PROCESS OUTSOURCING)

PETER DUCKER, an Australian born author once said “Do what you do the best and outsource the rest.” When your business starts to grow you or your staff cannot do certain things on your own, so at that stage outsourcing services from an efficient third party service provider is the best option you can jump onto as it is cost-effective and as well brings the expertise in your organization to carry on the work effectively.

(KPO) knowledge process outsourcing is a firm, that does the knowledge and information related work delegated by an organization in need of it. This involves high value work carried by highly skilled personnel, literal expert in their respective fields.
BACK OFFICE SERVICES FOR BANKING SSECTOR : Banking is one of the largest growing sector world-wide, and with growth comes the responsibility to manage all the operations very aptly and set up a structure to have aligned series of functions from database management to customers support and to get these things done experts are required but hiring that many experts can be a hectic and cost increasing task, so outsourcing is the best one can opt for.
Generally, there are no defined criteria to decide for which service to outsource, it is industry specific and for banking it’s back office work includes data entry and management, Account Servicing, Record Processes, Transaction Management, Regulatory compliance with the laws and regulations, IT & cyber security support for cyber attacks and hacks and Client Acquisition such as telemarketing, processing applications, customer verification, approval, documentation etc.

BACK OFFICE SERVICES FOR LAW FIRM : Back office services outsourced by a law firm are care of regulation, insurance, systems, compliance, legal cashiering for invoicing and credit control, handling client money, SRA audits, Company incorporation & administration services, Accounting services for your company and its directors, Recruitment services to help you find lawyers to join your firm, Branding, digital, web, social media and marketing, Financial services, financial planning and lending facilities, Banking, Legal resource on demand.

(CRM) CUSTOMER RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT : Customer relationship management is need of the hour for every industry to enhance and pamper its clientele to enjoy their loyalty for longer period of time but setting up this service in your own space can be a time consuming and an expensive affair.
Outsourcing of the CRM services is the most promising solution to meet increasing demand for standard CRM services and rising costs linked with it. The CRM industry is in the process of restructuring itself with the advancement of technology ads up new range service offerings such as modern communication and social media platforms. Customer relationship management (CRM) manages all your company’s relationships and interactions with potential customers and look after customer satisfaction to build a chain of loyal customers.

BACK OFFICE SERVICES FOR ACCOUNTS DEPARTMENTS : Accounting has been a service to be outsourced but at the same time accounting department also need few services to be outsourced for the ease of work such as After-the-fact accounts payable entry, Accounts payable, Accounts receivable, Payroll processing and filings, Account reconciliation for tax planning and filing, Technology solutions, payroll services, fund administration, finance, and accounting services, Human resources (HR) consulting and outsourcing, including HR support and help desk.

OUTSOURCING LEAD GENERATION : When you have other core functions in hand or unable to bring in the prospect leads or sales representatives have less than a year of experience, you outsource external expert companies to build your company’s sales funnel. Such companies do this by performing a wide range of lead generation activities including cold calling and product or service promotion for your company to bring in the best leads which serves the best interest for the business. As outsourcing is cost effective process so those Companies which are in favor to have monitory benefits often turn to outsourcing lead generation in order to get more bangs for their buck outsourcing layup more leads because of that third party’s advanced skills and familiarity.

OUTSOURCING LIVE TRANSACTION TRACKING : For the sectors such as e-commerce, crypto etc. dealing with the customers at the front end directly and tracking the transactions made online gets heavy on head because for the business to run smoothly a robust live transaction tracking arrangement is required to track the transaction as the payment is made but as said earlier it gets difficult and cost increasing for any business to hire and then train the resource for such a long period of time so outsourcing this service of live transaction tracking is one sane decision a business can make. Live transaction tracking system is a process where once a transaction is made, it is then broadcasted to the network for validation and permissions, then tracking of status in the ledger live application happen to know when it gets confirmed and when your balance gets updated.
